About

Chris

My name is Christoffer Skytte Wielsøe. I was born in 1995 in Køge but I now live in Copenhagen.

I enjoy many styles of art. I draw a lot of inspiration from the minimalism of far-eastern designs. I like to combine the simple design with the northern pastel palette.

During the last half year I have found a passion for coding and I love to find ways to make my designs come to life on the web.
